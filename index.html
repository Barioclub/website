The Full Story

As avid travelers, we often find ourselves reaching out to local friends for recommendations on the most authentic, unspoiled places to explore in cities around the world. This shared passion for discovering hidden gems and creating meaningful experiences has been central to the founders of Bario. Over time, we accumulated countless curated lists—scattered across various apps and formats—which inspired us to create something better.

Bario was born out of this vision: a multi-platform community where all our personalized, handpicked recommendations come together in one place.
